About Us

Who Are We?

 Our Mission

It's simple moments like dropping off the kids at soccer practice to road trips with your best friends that make cars integral to our world. With the necessity for cars growing over time, Safe Park Motors strives to connect with our community by offering them the best deals we can. In every department, our staff ensures that you and your car stay perfectly in sync. We're there with you every step of the way from the first turn of the car key to the last tire change.


 Our Start

We got our start in Goddard, Kansas just between 199th and 151st when two people decided that the area needed a better way to shop for cars. Throughout the years, we've dedicated ourselves to fostering our community through accesible and comprehensive service. Today, we serve the greater Wichita area in all their automotive needs.
